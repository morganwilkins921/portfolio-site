About Me

My Design Story

Hi, I’m Morgan, a UX/UI designer with a unique blend of experience in technical design and wellness. My journey began in apparel technical design, where I collaborated on high-profile collections at Under Armour. Through this work, I honed my skills in problem-solving, precision, and user-centered thinking—whether resolving garment fit issues or streamlining production processes. As a yoga instructor, I discovered the joy of building community and helping individuals achieve their wellness goals. Guiding students through mindfulness practices taught me the power of empathy and the importance of meeting diverse needs with care and creativity. Transitioning into UX design felt like a natural evolution, allowing me to combine my background in design and wellness to make an even greater impact. I thrive on tackling complex challenges, simplifying the complicated, and designing solutions that truly resonate. I’m especially drawn to health tech, wellness, and fashion tech projects where I can bridge the gap between functionality and a meaningful user experience. For me, great design isn’t just about aesthetics—it’s about creating experiences that leave people feeling seen, supported, and inspired.
